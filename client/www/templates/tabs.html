Creating a Hunt

In order to create a hunt, you must be signed in on your personal account. Hunts can be private, so that only you can start the game using that hunt. Public hunts are available to everyone!

Starting a Game

To start a scavenger hunt with your friends, go to Choose a Hunt and choose a hunt from the list available. Click on each hunt for a further description of what the hunt entails!

Once a hunt has been created, you will be given a game code, which you can give to friends so they can join your game. Click start once all your friends have joined and are listed in the game lobby!

Joining a Friend's Game

To join a game, one person in the group must have created a new game through Choose a Hunt. Enter the game code they were given and you will prompted for your team name and sent to the lobby of their game!
